# Arturo Orlacchio, PhD

Translational oncology scientist working at the interface of drug discovery, biomarker development, preclinical models, and data-driven experimental strategy.

I use computational and statistical tools to make experimental oncology more reproducible, interpretable, and decision-ready.

## Focus areas

- Translational oncology and precision medicine
- Small-molecule drug discovery and preclinical strategy
- Biomarker assay development and validation
- PDAC, NSCLC, chromatin biology, hormone signaling, and tumor microenvironment biology
- Dose-response analysis, survival analysis, omics interpretation, and study-design planning

## Selected technical themes

- Python/R-based analysis workflows
- Dose-response, drug-combination, and PK/PD modeling
- Biomarker assay QC, ROC / diagnostic performance, and reproducibility
- Statistical process control (Shewhart / Levey-Jennings charts)
- Kaplan-Meier and Cox survival analysis
- RNA-seq / ATAC-seq-style exploratory analysis and mutation-landscape summaries
- Clinical-trial design simulation, meta-analysis, and preclinical study planning
